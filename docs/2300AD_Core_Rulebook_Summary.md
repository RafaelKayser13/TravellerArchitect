# 2300AD Core Rulebook Index Summary

## Character Creation & Advancement

- **Characteristics:** STR, DEX, END, INT, EDU, SOC, PSI.
- **Skills:** Skill levels 0 to 4+. DMs based on characteristic/1.
- **Careers:** Assignment, Survival, Advancement, Events, Mishaps, Ranks/Bonuses.
- **Life Events:** Random table for significant background events.
- **Psionics:** PSI Strength, Institute Testing, Talents (Telepathy, Clairvoyance, Telekinesis, Awareness, Teleportation).

## Gameplay Mechanics

- **Task Checks:** 2D + Skill + Characteristic DM + Difficulty DM vs. Target (6-14+).
- **Combat:** Initiative (2D+DEX), Minor/Significant actions, Damage vs. Endurance/Characteristics.
- **Damage types:** Slug, Energy, Melee, Explosives (Blast, AP, Auto).
- **Healing:** Natural recovery (1+END/day), Medical care (First Aid, Surgery).
- **Environmental Dangers:** Radiation, Temperature, Vacuum, Poison, Disease.

## Equipment & Technology

- **Tech Levels (TL):** 0 to 16+, defining availability and capability.
- **Armor:** Protection scores (vs. damage subtraction), TL-dependant types.
- **Augments:** Cybernetics, Biological enhancements (STR, DEX, END, Intelligence).
- **Computer Systems:** Processing power, Software (Interface, Expert, Agent, Intellect).
- **Sensors:** Visual, Thermal, EM, Radar/Lidar, NAS, Densitometers.
- **Toolkits:** Specialized kits for Engineering, Medical, Science, etc.

## Vehicles & Spacecraft

- **Vehicles:** Speed Bands, Agility, Hull, Armor, Weapons.
- **Spacecraft Operations:** Fuel (Refined/Unrefined), Power Plant requirements, Jump Travel (100-diameter limit, Misjumps).
- **Spacecraft Construction:** Hull tonnage, Drives (M-Drive, J-Drive), Bridge, Computers, Sensors, Weapons (Turrets/Hardpoints).
- **Boarding Actions:** Combat between ships at close range.
- **Common Ships:** Scout/Courier (Type S), Free Trader (Type A), Mercenary Cruiser (Type C).

## Trade & Economy

- **Passenger Traffic:** High, Middle, Basic, Low passage categories.
- **Freight & Mail:** Shipping goods between worlds for fixed rates.
- **Speculative Trade:** Buying/Selling goods (D66 Trade Goods Table) based on Purchase/Sale DMs.
- **Black Market:** Illegal goods, Fixers, and Law Level impacts.

## World Creation

- **Subsectors/Sectors:** 8x10 hex grids, 1 parsec per hex.
- **World Profiles (UWP):** Size, Atmosphere, Hydrographics, Population, Government, Law Level, TL, Starport.
- **Bases:** Scout, Navy, Gas Giants (for refuelling).
- **Travel Zones:** Amber (Caution), Red (Prohibited).
